Our HISTORY

Our history

It all began in 1994. The pizzeria's founder, Marco, arrived
in the city with just a suitcase and an old starter his grandfather
Santino had saved from their time in Naples.
Marco didn't want to open a pretentious restaurant. He dreamed
of a place where a construction worker in a stained overall
and a businessman in an expensive tie could sit at adjacent tables, united
by the shared delight of their first bite.

Where are we located?

Welcome to Mamma Mia Pizza—the heart of
true flavor. We await you in the heart of Rome, where the aroma of fresh
basil meets Italian hospitality. Experience
the spirit of Italy in every bite of our legendary pizza, made with love. We are on Pizzeria Street, 42.

Your perfect Roman lunch starts here!

Our products

At Mamma Mia Pizza, quality isn't just a word, it's our philosophy. We use only
the finest ingredients: premium soft wheat flour, juicy tomatoes ripened in the Italian sun,
and the finest mozzarella. Every ingredient undergoes
strict quality control to ensure you enjoy the taste of authentic Roman
pizza. We believe that only the finest gifts of
nature can create a true culinary masterpiece worthy of your table.
